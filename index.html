Our history

For many years, Zeerah has been baking the most pies at her home. In 2013, we opened our first store in ijoko (Nigeria) in a cosy little street near the Grand Market People from all over the world fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies... The store became success in no time.

In 2015, not less than 100 pies were sold every day and Zeerah`s Pie Shop moved into a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this our very own website, making it possible to order Zeerah`s delicious pies whenever you feel like it!

Zeerah is famous for using only the best ingredient. This way, we can make sure that our pies are absolutely the best.
